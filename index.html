About Christopher A. Welsh

After serving in the United States Air Force for 4 years, Chris realized that serving, and defending others was his calling in life. That was when he made the decision to become a lawyer.


He went to school at the University of California, Santa Barbara, graduating in 2007 with High Honors. He then moved to the Bay Area to attend Santa Clara University, School of Law, graduating in 2010.


While in law school, he saw the criminal justice system up close and in action as a Judicial Intern at the Santa Clara Superior Court, in a misdemeanor department. After that experience, he knew that he could make a difference helping others to navigate the criminal justice system.

At the Law Office of Christopher A. Welsh, clients are treated with respect and dignity. We are dedicated to making sure that all clients receive our best efforts, in order to aggressively defend their rights. Attorney Christopher A. Welsh has worked with some of the most trusted and experienced defense attorneys in the Northern California Bay Area, and he is ready to help you. We want to fight for you.
